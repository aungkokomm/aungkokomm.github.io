Bucket

A staging shelf for moving files around Windows. Gather files, folders, text, images and links from anywhere into a little always-on-top bucket — then copy, move, zip, or drag them out wherever you need.

⬇ Download v1.0.0 View source
C# / WinUI 3 · .NET 10 · Portable · MIT licensed

Why Bucket

🪣 Collect anything

Drag in, paste, or use the Add button — files, folders, text snippets, images, and links. Text, images and links are saved as real files for you.

🤙 Summon it anywhere

Shake the mouse to pop a bucket under your cursor, press Ctrl+Shift+B, or drop onto the optional screen-edge catcher.

🎨 Many buckets

Open as many as you like — each its own colour, nameable, with compact and expanded square modes that toggle with a smooth animation.

📤 Deposit in one move

Copy To… / Move To… any folder with conflict handling and progress, pin Quick Destinations, or drag straight out to Explorer or any app (Shift = move).

🗜 Export transforms

Copy the whole bucket as a .zip, flatten subfolders into one place, or batch-rename and copy with a sequential prefix.

🔒 Safe & private

Adding an item only stores a reference — nothing is copied, moved, or deleted until you say so. System-tray resident, portable, no telemetry.
