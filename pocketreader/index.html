PocketReader

Pocket is gone — but your reading list shouldn't be. PocketReader turns your Raindrop.io bookmarks into a fast, private, fully-offline reading library that you actually own.

⬇ Download v1.8.2 View source
C# / WinUI 3 · .NET 8 · Portable · MIT licensed

Why PocketReader

📴 Truly offline

Caches the full article text and images to your disk. Read, search, and highlight with no connection — and it outlives link-rot and paywalls.

🔎 Full-text search

Millisecond search across your whole offline library — not just titles and tags, but the words inside every cached article.

🖊 Highlights & notes

Highlight text right in the reader and jot per-article notes. Everything is saved locally and reappears every time you open the article.

🔊 Listen & export

Built-in offline text-to-speech reads any article aloud, and one click exports it to a clean PDF.

⭐ Organize & track

Star ratings, a drag-and-drop tag manager, reading progress with resume, a Recycle Bin, and a statistics page for what you've read.

🔒 One-way & private

Syncs from Raindrop and never writes back. Portable single folder, no server, no subscription, no telemetry.
