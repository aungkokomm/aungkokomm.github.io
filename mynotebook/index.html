My Notebook

A fast, private, local-first notebook for Windows 11 — a two-pane workspace for rich notes and OCR-searchable screenshot threads, with a Myanmar-aware search that actually finds what you wrote. No account, no cloud, no telemetry. Your notes stay yours.

⬇ Download v1.8.0 View source
C# / WinUI 3 · .NET 8 · Portable · MIT licensed

Why My Notebook

🔒 Truly yours

Everything lives in a portable Data/ folder next to the app — copy it to a USB stick, back it up as a .zip, sync it your own way. No sign-in, no cloud, no telemetry.

🔎 Search that finds it

A hybrid full-text + trigram index finds a word in the middle of spaceless Myanmar text, partial words, and even the text inside your screenshots — with operators, ranking, and jump-to-match.

🖼 Screenshot threads

Paste a screenshot and it lands inline at full resolution. Every one is OCR-indexed, drag-to-reorder, opens in a 100% floating viewer, and exports in a click.

🗂 Two-pane workspace

A joined folders + note-list drawer you can collapse either side of, rich note rows, multi-select for bulk move/pin/delete, drag-to-folder, and a Timeline to browse everything by date.

✍️ A real editor

Headings, lists, checklists, highlight, color, fonts, and page styles. Paste a whole web article and keep the formatting and images — pulled in so the note stays offline — plus [[wiki-links]] and backlinks.

🎨 Make it yours

Light/dark plus eight color themes that recolor the whole UI with an intensity dial, custom page colors and rule lines, and a distraction-free focus mode.

📤 Export, voice & safety

Export to PDF, Word .docx, or a self-contained web page. Read notes aloud, record audio, and rely on automatic version history with restore and .zip backup.
